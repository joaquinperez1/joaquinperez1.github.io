Los Sueños Ácidos del Apocalipsis

"Los Sueños Acidos del Apocalipsis" (Acid Dreams of the Apocalypse) is a collection of electronic psychedelic tracks I made this year while living in isolation, sleeping during the day and making music at night fueled by mixed berry smoothies and mushroom microdoses.
I was inspired by psychedelic music, retro sci-fi and surrealist art and diverse artists such as Alice Coltrane, Dora Jar, Legowelt, J Dilla, Tame Impala.
This is the first EP I release under Copperz but it's not the first one I made, I was supposed to release other two albums this year but times changed and I decided to release this independently as a way to capture and share the sublime and melancholic vibrations that I felt this year, which to me and many people felt like the beginning of the apocalypse.

to hear the EP click on the following link Los Sueños Acidos del Apocalipsis


Copperz is the alter ego I use for my most personal and free music productions, the project was born in Brooklyn, New York in 2017 since then I have played in Boston, New York, LA, Buenos Aires both as a DJ and with a live ensemble.
